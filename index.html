Find the hidden transformation rule

Guess the rule transforming each word into the next. You get 6 tries.

How to play

Each pair of words follows a hidden rule.

Your goal: figure out the transformation.

  • You get 6 guesses
  • Each wrong guess reveals another example
  • The rule is always consistent
SMILE → MILES
PLATE → LATEP

Example rule: rotate letters left by 1
